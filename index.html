Haru Urara

Haru Urara was a Japanese racehorse who became famous for her long losing streak of 113 consecutive losses, which earned her a cult following in Japan. Despite losing every race she entered from 1996 to 2004, Haru Urara's perseverance and spirit captured the hearts of many fans, making her a symbol of hope and determination. Her story highlights the emotional connection people can have with animals, even in the face of repeated failure.
